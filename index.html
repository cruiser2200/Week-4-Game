Crystal Collector Game!

HOW TO PLAY


You will be given a random number at the beginning of each game.

There are four crystals displayed below. By selecting a specific crystal, you will be adding points to your number.

You win the game if your number exactly matches the random number. You lose the game if your number exceeds the random nummber.
